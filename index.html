Revenue · Pricing Strategy
Are we losing volume and rate because of us — or because of the market?
Distinguishes a hotel-specific problem from market-wide softness — so your team never cuts rates when the market doesn't require it, and never holds them when it does.

Revenue · Groups & Sales

Analyses your pipeline against last year, identifies lost business patterns by competitor and reason, and gives your sales team a specific recovery plan — not a general directive to "do more".
Converts lost business data into a prioritised sales action list
