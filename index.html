Is our forward pace a pricing problem — or a demand problem?
Tracks 30, 60 and 90-day OTB against last year by segment. Distinguishes a hotel-specific issue from market-wide softness — so your team never cuts rates when the market doesn't require it, and never holds them when it does.

Data Security

Your property data is
yours. Always.

Hotel commercial data is sensitive. We treat it that way.

🔒
Isolated by property
Every hotel's data is stored in a dedicated, isolated environment. Row-level security enforced at database level. No property can access another's data — by architecture, not policy.
🚫
Never shared. Never sold.
Your STR, PMS, and pipeline data is used solely to generate your property's analysis. It is never shared with third parties, never aggregated for benchmarking products, and never used to train models on your behalf without consent.
🛡️
Encrypted in transit and at rest
All data is encrypted end-to-end. Connections are secured over HTTPS. Storage is encrypted at rest. Access is controlled by authenticated sessions with no shared credentials across properties.
